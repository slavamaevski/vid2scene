The Challenge with Creating Photorealistic 3D Environments

Current solutions for creating photorealistic 3D environments often require expensive hardware. This makes the process inaccessible to many creators and businesses. vid2scene is a free online Gaussian Splatting platform that leverages recent advances in computer graphics research to provide a software-only approach, allowing anybody to create photorealistic 3D scenes from simple 2D videos.

How It Works

Record

Record a video of your environment. You can use any device that has a camera, including your smartphone.

Upload

Seamlessly upload your video to our platform. We will process it and generate a photorealistic 3D scene using our free online Gaussian Splatting technology.

Explore

Explore your immersive 3D scene in real-time using our web-based 3D viewer. VR support is coming soon!

Advantages

No Specialized Hardware Required

All you need is a smartphone to create stunning 3D scenes. No expensive camera equipment required.

Unconstrained, Moveable Viewpoint

You can explore your scene from any angle or position within the environment. Other solutions are limited to fixed points-of-view.

Realistic Lighting and Reflections

Current 3D scanning technology provides a static look, without any dynamic light reflections. vid2scene uses realistic lighting and reflections for free and by default.

Real-Time Rendering on Any Device

Experience your 3D scenes seamlessly across all your devices.
