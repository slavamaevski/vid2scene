Responsible AI Strategy

vid2scene's responsible AI approach focuses on three core principles that guide the development of vid2scene:

Privacy by Design

We minimize data collection by only storing what's necessary for scene generation and anonymous usage metrics through Umami analytics. Users maintain full control of their content with simple deletion options.

Environmental Responsibility

We're actively optimizing our scene generation algorithms to reduce processing time and energy consumption, with a commitment to monitor and reduce our carbon footprint as we scale.

Accessibility and Inclusion

Our web application follows accessibility best practices, including screen reader compatibility and keyboard navigation alternatives, with regular evaluations to improve usability for all abilities.

As a small, early-stage company, our responsible AI strategy is evolving alongside our technology. We're committed to integrating best practices from industry frameworks like NIST's AI Risk Management Framework as we grow, and we welcome feedback from users and partners to help shape our approach.
